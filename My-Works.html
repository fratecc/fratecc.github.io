Agar.io in Processing

I made this for school in 2019. It's not fantastic, but i worked hard to make the "two player, two screen" work.
You can play this game by lounching it and clicking on the "main sketch" page.
You will be able to use the cursor to move a sphere and WASD to move another.
This project has been very challenging to me at the time thanks to the dual screen (which is not one of the best things you can do on processing).
